What your assistant can help with

Start with the customer questions and lead details that slow you down every day.

LoneStar AI focuses on practical business workflows first: FAQs, lead intake, quote preparation, follow-up, website chat, and business knowledge organization.

Customer-facing

Answer questions and collect info

A practical front door for customers who need answers before they call, text, or request a quote.

  • Customer FAQ Assistant — answers common questions from approved business info.
  • Lead Intake Assistant — gathers names, phone numbers, service needs, photos needed, and job details.
  • Website Chat Assistant — helps website visitors understand services and request a follow-up.
  • Quote Prep Helper — asks the questions needed before the owner reviews pricing or scheduling.

Owner-facing

Keep work organized

Support for the owner or manager so messages, leads, and next steps are easier to track.

  • Customer Reply Drafts — drafts text, email, review, and quote responses for approval.
  • Lead Tracking — turns calls, texts, emails, and web leads into organized tracker rows.
  • Follow-Up Helper — creates reminders or draft messages for leads, quotes, and appointments.
  • Daily Summary — summarizes leads, questions, appointments, and next steps.

Advanced / local

Special workflows by quote

Some features depend on accounts, integrations, office computers, local files, phone tools, or hardware.

  • Internal Document Search — answers staff questions from approved manuals, notes, policies, and FAQs.
  • AI Phone Answering — call intake, routing, and approved answers after careful testing.
  • Local Machine Setup — customer-owned Mac/Linux or approved mini PC for local features.
  • Custom Integrations — quoted after reviewing access, risk, and complexity.

Local hardware and advanced integrations are available later, but they are not the default starting point.
