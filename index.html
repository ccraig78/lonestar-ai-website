Feature menu

Start with one practical feature. Add more when the business is ready.

The $500 starter setup includes the managed VPS, OpenClaw installation, basic business setup, and one VPS-Beginner feature. Advanced and Local features are priced separately because they depend on accounts, integrations, access, and risk.

VPS-Beginner

Best first features

Cloud-friendly, human-approved workflows that usually do not require deep system access.

  • Customer FAQ Assistant — answers common questions from approved business info.
  • Email Assistant — drafts replies and summarizes copied or read-only email.
  • Lead Inquiry Tracking — turns calls, texts, emails, and web leads into organized tracker rows.
  • Website Content Update Assistant — drafts page updates, FAQs, calls-to-action, and service descriptions.
  • Social Media Draft Assistant — turns jobs, photos, reviews, and announcements into post drafts.
  • Review Response Draft Assistant — drafts professional replies to Google/Facebook/Yelp reviews.
  • Simple Reminder / Check-In Workflow — sends scheduled reminders, follow-up prompts, or daily check-ins.
  • Local SEO / Service Page Assistant — reviews the website and drafts local service pages or SEO improvements.

VPS-Advanced

Deeper cloud workflows

More powerful features that may need OAuth, forms, calendars, spreadsheets, phone tools, or multiple business systems.

  • Internal Document Search Assistant — answers staff questions from approved manuals, policies, notes, and FAQs.
  • AI Phone Answering Assistant — call intake, basic approved answers, routing, and escalation after careful testing.
  • Appointment Scheduling / Reminder Assistant — collects appointment requests and drafts scheduling/reminder messages.
  • Quote / Estimate Drafting Assistant — gathers job details and drafts estimates for owner review.
  • Customer Follow-Up Sequence — creates follow-up drafts/reminders for leads, quotes, appointments, or completed jobs.
  • Job Intake Assistant — collects structured customer/job info for quoting, scheduling, or follow-up.
  • Website Contact Form Lead Routing — routes form submissions into summaries, notifications, lead rows, or draft replies.
  • Multi-Agent Role Workflow — separates roles such as receptionist, estimator, marketing helper, and manager summary.
  • Business Dashboard / Report Summary — daily or weekly summaries of leads, emails, appointments, and follow-ups.

Local

Consultation required

For workflows that need a customer-owned Mac/Linux machine, local files, office devices, LAN-only systems, or on-site computer access.

  • Business Document Organizer — organizes approved local folders, forms, manuals, photos, and business files.
  • Photo / Job Summary Generation — turns local job photos and notes into internal summaries, customer updates, or marketing drafts.
  • Local machine setup — dedicated Mac, Linux PC, or approved mini PC configuration.
  • Device or office integration — printer, scanner, LAN-only software, or office-computer workflows.
  • On-site support package — priced after reviewing the machine, access needs, and risk.

Local setup is available, but pricing is discussed after a consultation. Windows is not the preferred backend for production assistant installs.
