Every LoneStar AI assistant starts with a custom foundation and one included starter feature. From there, you can add individual features or choose a value pack if you want several workflows set up together.

Starter features

Simple customer help

Good first features for answering repeat questions, sharing business basics, and capturing simple requests.

  • FAQ Assistant — answers common questions from approved business info.
  • Business Info Assistant — explains services, hours, location, service area, and next steps.
  • Callback Request Helper — collects name, phone, preferred time, and reason for contact.
  • Website Chat Starter — gives website visitors a practical first place to ask questions.

One starter feature is included with base setup. Additional starter features usually start around $150–$250.

Standard workflows

Lead capture and follow-up

For businesses that want the assistant to collect better details, prepare quote info, and help organize next steps.

  • Lead Intake Assistant — gathers customer details, service needs, photos needed, and job notes.
  • Quote Prep Helper — asks the questions needed before the owner reviews pricing or scheduling.
  • Follow-Up Helper — drafts reminders and messages for leads, quotes, and appointments.
  • Lead Tracking — turns customer requests into cleaner tracker rows and summaries.

Standard workflow features usually start around $300–$500 depending on setup depth.

Advanced / quoted

Special workflows and integrations

Some features depend on business accounts, files, phone systems, websites, APIs, office computers, or hardware.

  • Internal Document Search — helps staff find answers from approved manuals, notes, policies, and FAQs.
  • AI Phone Answering — call intake and routing after careful testing and guardrails.
  • Local Machine Setup — customer-owned Mac/Linux or approved mini PC for local features.
  • Custom Integrations — quoted after reviewing access, risk, and complexity.

Advanced work is quoted after review so we do not overpromise or underprice support-heavy setup.
